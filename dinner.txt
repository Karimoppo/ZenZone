1. Baked Salmon with Asparagus
Ingredients:

4 oz salmon fillet
1 cup asparagus spears
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 lemon wedge
Salt and pepper to taste
Calories: ~350

Instructions:

Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
Place salmon on a baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper.
Arrange asparagus around the salmon, drizzle with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper.
Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until salmon flakes easily and asparagus is tender.
Squeeze lemon juice over salmon before serving.
2. Chicken Stir-Fry with Vegetables
Ingredients:

4 oz chicken breast, sliced
1 cup mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, snap peas)
1 tablespoon olive oil
2 tablespoons low-sodium soy sauce
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1/2 cup cooked brown rice
Calories: ~350

Instructions:

Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
Add chicken and cook until no longer pink.
Add garlic and mixed vegetables, stir-fry for 5-7 minutes.
Add soy sauce and stir to combine.
Serve over cooked brown rice.
3. Quinoa-Stuffed Bell Peppers
Ingredients:

2 large bell peppers
1/2 cup cooked quinoa
1/2 cup black beans
1/4 cup corn kernels
1/4 cup diced tomatoes
1/4 cup shredded cheese (optional)
Spices to taste (cumin, paprika)
Calories: ~300

Instructions:

Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove seeds.
Mix cooked quinoa, black beans, corn, and diced tomatoes in a bowl. Season with spices.
Stuff the bell peppers with the quinoa mixture.
Place in a baking dish and bake for 25-30 minutes, topping with cheese in the last 5 minutes if desired.
4. Turkey and Spinach Stuffed Zucchini Boats
Ingredients:

2 medium zucchinis, halved lengthwise
4 oz ground turkey
1 cup fresh spinach, chopped
1/4 cup diced onion
1/4 cup marinara sauce
1/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
Calories: ~320

Instructions:

Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
Scoop out the center of each zucchini half to create boats.
Cook ground turkey and onion in a pan until browned. Add spinach and cook until wilted.
Mix in marinara sauce and spoon the mixture into the zucchini boats.
Top with mozzarella cheese and bake for 20-25 minutes.
5. Shrimp and Vegetable Skewers
Ingredients:

4 oz shrimp, peeled and deveined
1 cup mixed vegetables (bell peppers, onions, zucchini)
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 tablespoon lemon juice
Salt, pepper, and herbs to taste
Calories: ~250

Instructions:

Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
Toss shrimp and vegetables with olive oil, lemon juice, salt, pepper, and herbs.
Thread shrimp and vegetables onto skewers.
Grill for 2-3 minutes per side, until shrimp are cooked through and vegetables are tender.
6. Lentil and Veggie Stew
Ingredients:

1 cup cooked lentils
1 cup diced tomatoes
1/2 cup carrots, diced
1/2 cup celery, diced
1/4 cup onion, chopped
1 cup vegetable broth
1 tablespoon olive oil
Calories: ~300

Instructions:

Heat olive oil in a pot over medium heat. Sauté onions, carrots, and celery until softened.
Add diced tomatoes, lentils, and vegetable broth.
Simmer for 20-25 minutes until vegetables are tender and flavors are blended.
7. Chicken and Broccoli Stir-Fry
Ingredients:

4 oz chicken breast, sliced
1 cup broccoli florets
1/2 cup sliced bell peppers
1 tablespoon olive oil
2 tablespoons low-sodium soy sauce
1 teaspoon ginger, minced
1/2 cup cooked quinoa
Calories: ~350

Instructions:

Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
Add chicken and cook until browned.
Add ginger, broccoli, and bell peppers, stir-fry for 5-7 minutes.
Add soy sauce and cook for an additional 2 minutes.
Serve over cooked quinoa.
