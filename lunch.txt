1. Turkey and Veggie Lettuce Wraps
Ingredients:

1 pound ground turkey
1 red bell pepper (diced)
1 cup mushrooms (diced)
2 cloves garlic (minced)
2 tablespoons soy sauce
1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
1 tablespoon sesame oil
Lettuce leaves (e.g., butter or Romaine)
Instructions:

In a pan, heat sesame oil over medium heat. Add garlic and cook until fragrant.
Add ground turkey and cook until browned.
Add bell pepper, mushrooms, soy sauce, and hoisin sauce. Cook until veggies are tender.
Serve the turkey mixture in lettuce leaves as wraps.

2. Spinach and Feta Stuffed Chicken Breast
Ingredients:

4 chicken breasts
1 cup fresh spinach (chopped)
1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes (chopped)
1 tablespoon olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:

Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
Mix spinach, feta, and sun-dried tomatoes in a bowl.
Make a pocket in each chicken breast and stuff with the spinach mixture.
Secure with toothpicks, season with salt and pepper, and place in a baking dish.
Drizzle with olive oil and bake for 25-30 minutes until cooked through.

3. Roasted Veggie and Hummus Wrap
Ingredients:

1 zucchini (sliced)
1 red bell pepper (sliced)
1 cup cherry tomatoes (halved)
1 tablespoon olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste
Whole wheat tortilla
1/4 cup hummus
Instructions:

Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
Toss zucchini, bell pepper, and cherry tomatoes with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Spread on a baking sheet.
Roast for 20-25 minutes until veggies are tender.
Spread hummus on the tortilla, add roasted veggies, and roll up.

4. Cabbage and Carrot Slaw with Apple Cider Vinegar Dressing
Ingredients:

2 cups shredded cabbage
1 cup shredded carrots
1/4 cup chopped green onions
1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
1 tablespoon honey
1 tablespoon olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:

In a large bowl, mix cabbage, carrots, and green onions.
In a small bowl, whisk together apple cider vinegar, honey, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
Pour dressing over the slaw and toss well.

5. Sweet Potato and Black Bean Burrito Bowl
Ingredients:

1 large sweet potato (diced)
1 can black beans (rinsed and drained)
1/2 cup cooked brown rice or quinoa
1/2 avocado (sliced)
1/4 cup salsa
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 teaspoon cumin
Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:

Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss sweet potato with olive oil, cumin, salt, and pepper.
Roast sweet potato for 25-30 minutes until tender.
In a bowl, combine roasted sweet potato, black beans, brown rice or quinoa, avocado, and salsa.

6. Mango and Black Bean Quinoa Salad
Ingredients:

1 cup cooked quinoa
1 cup black beans (rinsed and drained)
1 ripe mango (diced)
1/2 red onion (diced)
1/4 cup chopped cilantro
Juice of 1 lime
Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:

In a bowl, combine quinoa, black beans, mango, red onion, and cilantro.
Drizzle with lime juice and season with salt and pepper. Toss to combine.

7. Zucchini Noodles with Pesto
Ingredients:

2 large zucchinis (spiralized into noodles)
1/2 cup pesto sauce (store-bought or homemade)
1/4 cup cherry tomatoes (halved)
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
Instructions:

In a large skillet, lightly sauté zucchini noodles over medium heat until just tender, about 2-3 minutes.
Remove from heat and toss with pesto sauce and cherry tomatoes.
Top with Parmesan cheese if desired.
These recipes are not only healthy but also full of flavor and variety. Enjoy your lunch!
